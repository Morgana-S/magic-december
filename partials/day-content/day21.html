Don’t Make Your Bed Day

Embrace the delightful chaos of Don’t Make Your Bed Day on December 21st, a whimsical holiday that encourages you to ditch the chores and revel in the joy of a messy bed! Discover the charming story behind this fun celebration and learn how you can join in on the rebellion against tidiness for a day of carefree relaxation!

Don’t Make Your Bed Day, celebrated on December 21st, is undoubtedly one of the most amusing holidays around, and it all started with an adorable petition from a fifth-grader in New Mexico! If you’ve ever felt the urge to rebel against the tidy demands of adulthood, this day is your perfect excuse to embrace the delightful chaos of an unmade bed and enjoy a little carefree fun. How to Celebrate Don’t Make Your Bed Day For many children—and surprisingly, quite a few adults—not making the bed is a playful act of defiance against the structured world of grown-ups. Here are some fun ways to join in the celebration:

  • Skip Making Your Bed (and Other Chores!): Why stop at just leaving your bed unmade? Take a break from other mundane tasks too—perhaps skip your morning coffee or let your hair be a bit wild today! Don’t Make Your Bed Day is all about relaxation and breaking free from those pesky “rules” that govern our lives.
  • Treat Yourself to a New Bed: If you’ve been thinking about upgrading your sleeping situation, now’s the perfect time! Use this day as an excuse to invest in a new bed that promises comfort and style.
  • Invest in Easy Bedding: Say goodbye to complicated bed-making rituals! Consider getting a duvet set that makes it a breeze—just throw the duvet on top and you’re done!
  • Share the Fun on Social Media: Celebrate your messy bed by posting photos online and spreading the word about Don’t Make Your Bed Day. It’s refreshing to see a bit of chaos in our perfectly curated social media feeds!

A Bit of History

Don’t Make Your Bed Day is relatively new, having been inspired by a charming petition written by fifth-grader Shannon Barba. He expressed his desire for a day where no one had to make their beds, arguing that everyone deserves a break from this daily chore. His heartfelt plea resonated with many, highlighting that even adults sometimes feel tired of tidying up! So, whether you’re a neat freak or someone who enjoys a bit of messiness, Don’t Make Your Bed Day is an excellent opportunity to embrace imperfection and enjoy life’s little pleasures. After all, who wouldn’t want an excuse to relax in their unmade bed for just one day?
